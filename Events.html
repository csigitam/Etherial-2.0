Ethereal 2.O Events

CSI Ethereal 2.O brings to you a variety of technical and non-technical events. Read on to learn more!

Technical Events

Mock Placements

This gives an overall experience of the placement process. In association with the Directorate of training and placements.

Brain Twister

The fun game Twister with a tinge of coding associated with it fulfilling the motto of learning through fun.

Inventa

A series of coding puzzles where the answer to every puzzle combines to form another puzzle. The winner is decided by the person closest to the solution.

Bug's Eye

Aim for the Bull’s Eye in the darts to score extra time for debugging the codes given.The closer you get to it, the more time you gain.

Code Painter

A Java-exclusive designing contest where the participants draw a picture with the given limited set of commands.

Hackathon

A coding event to complete the given incomplete programs, keeping in mind the practicality and significance in real-world problems.

Non-Technical Events

Treasure Hunt

Work together, use the clues to solve the problems faster and win the prize.

Pic-O-Mania

A fun game played in teams of two where one draws a picture and the other tries to guess it.

Nostalgic 90's

The 90’s cartoon themed dumb charades to stir up some fun.

Lost(Escape Rooms)

A model of Escape room wherein the participant has to face unique challenges to come out of the room
